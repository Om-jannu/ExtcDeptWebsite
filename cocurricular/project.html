Project Exhibition



Don Bosco Institute of Technology organizes a two day project exhibition and intra department competition ‘Innovex’ wherein the BE students from all four departments exhibit and demonstrate the projects developed by them under the guidance of respective faculty members. The exhibition is organized for the students to showcase their year long effort and it includes a total display and demonstration of 100 projects. The students exhibit their working models to various industrialists and academicians who come as judges and guests and provide a platform for open-ended discussions and deliberations for improving the scope of the project. The students are also encouraged to pitch in their ideas & solutions in front of distinguished guests. 2021 onwards, Innovex projects are being showcased at the official website of Innovex https://innovex.dbit.in
